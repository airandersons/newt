Victory Bible Institute

Rooted in truth, trained for service

Equipping labourers for the harvest

For over 15 years, NTEC Bible School has been training men and women for ministry — lay leaders, pastors, missionaries, and every believer who desires to know God deeply.

Our certificate and diploma programs combine rigorous biblical study with practical ministry exposure. Classes are held in the evenings and weekends to accommodate working professionals. You don’t need a theological background — just a hunger for the Word.

Programs offered

Certificate in Biblical Studies

One-year foundation course covering Old & New Testament, Hermeneutics, and Spiritual Formation. Perfect for every believer.

Diploma in Theology

Two-year in-depth program: systematic theology, church history, homiletics, and pastoral care. Ideal for ministry leaders.

Lay Leadership Track

Short courses for cell leaders, ushers, and ministry volunteers: discipleship, counseling basics, and servant leadership.

Mission & Evangelism

Practical training for outreach, cross-cultural ministry, and church planting. Includes local field assignments.

Why enroll at NTEC Bible School?

Experienced faculty

Learn from pastors, theologians, and ministry practitioners with decades of experience.

Flexible schedules

Evening and weekend classes so you can study while working or serving.

Practical ministry

Internships within NTEC ministries: preach, lead, counsel — real hands-on training.
