Our Ministries

Serving God, serving the city — there's a place for you.

Get involved · use your gifts

Choir

Lift your voice in harmony. Our choir leads worship and ministers through song every Sunday and special events.

Worship Team

Instrumentalists and vocalists creating an atmosphere of praise. Rehearsals weekly.

Youth Ministry

Empowering the next generation. Friday gatherings, camps, and mentorship.

Prison Ministry

Bringing hope and restoration to inmates in Luzira and other facilities. Monthly visits.

Evangelism Team

Sharing the good news in markets, schools, and door-to-door across Kampala.

Sunday School

Age-appropriate Bible lessons for children (3–12) during Sunday service.

Cell Groups

Weekly home gatherings for fellowship, prayer, and discipleship.

Join a Cell Group Near You

Experience church in a home. Cell groups meet weekly for prayer, Bible study, and community. It’s the best way to connect and grow.

Active areas in Kampala:

  • Kitintale– Mondays 7pm
  • Luzira – Tuesdays 6:30pm
  • Mutungo – Wednesdays 7pm
  • Najjanankumbi – Thursdays 7pm
  • Kunya – Fridays 6pm
  • Bwebajja – Saturdays 4pm

How to join:

Simply contact the church office or speak to any pastor after Sunday service. You can also fill the contact form and indicate “Cell Group”. A leader will reach out within 2 days.

Ready to serve?

Whatever your passion, there’s a ministry waiting for you. Fill in the short interest form, and we’ll connect you.
