About Our Church

Rooted in Kampala, reaching the world

Three Decades of Faith & Impact

For over 30 years, New Testament Evangelical Church has been a beacon of hope in Kampala. What began as a small home fellowship is now a thriving community of 300+ members, 5 pastors, and over 50 ministers.

Through seasons of joy and challenge, we have witnessed God's faithfulness—planting churches, discipling leaders, and transforming lives through the power of the Word. Our history is a story of grace, growth, and a deep commitment to the Gospel.

Today, we stand on that foundation, reaching out to every corner of the city with the love of Christ.

Our Vision

To see Kampala and the nations transformed by the power of God's Word, raising generations of disciples who make disciples.

Our Mission

To worship God in spirit and truth, equip believers for ministry, and proclaim the Gospel through word and action in every community.

Cell Groups Across Kampala

We believe in church without walls. Our cell groups meet in homes throughout Kampala — from Kawempe to Makindye, Bwaise to Buziga. Each group is a place of prayer, fellowship, and practical care.

Whether you live in the city center or the suburbs, there’s a NTEC cell near you. It’s where life-changing relationships are built.
