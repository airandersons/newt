Welcome to NTEC

For over 30 years, we have been a family of faith in the heart of Kampala, raising disciples and transforming our community through the love of Christ.

Whether you are searching for hope, looking for a church home, or want to grow in your walk with God, you are welcome here. Join us as we experience the power of the Word together.

From the heart of our Senior Pastor

"Our vision is simple: to see Kampala and beyond transformed by the Gospel. For three decades, God has been faithful. Today, we are more than 300 strong, yet every person matters. You matter to God, and you matter to us. Come, let us walk this journey of faith together."

— Bishop Peter Mutebi

Senior Pastor, NTEC Kampala
